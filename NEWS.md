flexCausal 0.1.0
=================

- Initial CRAN release providing doubly robust one-step and TMLE estimators for ADMGs with latent variables.
- Bundled graph functions (`make.graph()`, `f.parents()`, etc.) to streamline identifiability checks.
- Added simulated datasets (`data_example_a`, `data_example_b`, `data_backdoor`) to reproduce README/vignette examples.
